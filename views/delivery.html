DELIVERY POLICY

COD Charges Policy

If you don't want to use online purcahse you can use COD.
We are not giving any discount on COD purchase.
We are giving free delivery above 500/- purchase. Below 500 we ae taking 50 rupees as a delivery charge.
We are accepting only Indian currency in COD purchase.

ONLINE Charges Policy

You can ONLINE purcahse instead of Cod.

What is the estimated delivery time?

For WEST BENGAL Location we will deliver within 2-3 days.

Before your delivery we will send a sms to your registered mobile no.

Please note may be for certain order we will cancel your order due to insufficient stock. For that case If you purchase through ONLINE we will return amount to your bank account from where you purchased within 3 days.

Cancellations by the Customer

You have to cancel before your delivery would be shipped. For ONLINE purchase we will return your paid amount to your Bank Account from where you purchased within 7 days.
